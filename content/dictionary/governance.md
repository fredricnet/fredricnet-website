# Governance

**Governance** refers to the frameworks, policies, and processes through which decisions are made, authority is exercised, and accountability is maintained. In the context of AI and autonomous systems, governance encompasses both organizational oversight and the technical mechanisms that ensure responsible and transparent operation.

## AI Governance

AI governance specifically addresses:

- **Ethical guidelines**: Principles for fair, transparent, and accountable AI
- **Regulatory compliance**: Adherence to laws and standards governing AI use
- **Risk management**: Identifying and mitigating risks from AI deployment
- **Transparency**: Making AI decision-making processes understandable and auditable

## Importance

Effective governance is essential for building trust in autonomous systems, ensuring alignment with human values, and managing the societal impact of AI-driven transformation.
