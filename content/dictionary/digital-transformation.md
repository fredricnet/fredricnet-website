# Digital Transformation

**Digital transformation** is the process of integrating digital technology into all areas of an organization, fundamentally changing how it operates, delivers value to stakeholders, and adapts to market demands. It goes beyond simply adopting new tools -- it involves rethinking processes, culture, and business models.

## Core Dimensions

- **Technology**: Adoption of cloud, AI, automation, and data platforms
- **Process**: Redesigning workflows and operations for efficiency
- **Culture**: Fostering a mindset of continuous innovation and adaptability
- **Customer experience**: Using digital channels to improve engagement and service

## Relationship to AI

AI and autonomous systems are key enablers of digital transformation, providing capabilities such as predictive analytics, intelligent automation, and personalized experiences at scale.
