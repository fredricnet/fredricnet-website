## Potential Benefits and Risks

Every transformative technology arrives carrying both promise and peril, and autonomous businesses are no exception. The potential benefits are staggering — systems that operate continuously, scale without human bottlenecks, and make decisions at machine speed. But the risks are equally profound — systemic fragility, wealth concentration, and the erosion of human agency in economic systems.

This chapter examines both sides with clear eyes, drawing on economic analysis, historical precedent, and the technical realities established in previous chapters.
